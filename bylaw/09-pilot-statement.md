## 8. A pilot for direct democracy

The drafting of this bylaw was conducted in public. The full text, every parameter, every section of prose, and the record of every suggested change, accepted or rejected, are available at:

> github.com/mmward9/london-council-pay-bylaw

This was a pilot. The councillor responsible for this bylaw commits to using the same public-drafting process for every substantive motion they bring forward during the term of office to which this bylaw applies.

Direct democracy does not replace representative democracy. It complements it. A council that drafts its motions in public is a council that has nothing to hide and has the discipline to defend every choice it makes.
