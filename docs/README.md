# Audit Trail for the PACT Bylaw

This directory contains the working documents that produced the current operative bylaw text. Each file is a primary-source artefact of the design, audit, or political record. They are kept here for transparency: every reader of this repository can trace why the bylaw reads the way it reads.

| File | What it is |
|---|---|
| [v12-proposal.md](v12-proposal.md) | The Pay Accountable to City Taxpayers (PACT) Bylaw Refactor Proposal, version 12. Twelve iterations of design work. Replaces an earlier "standalone bylaw" design with a single-clause amending by-law to the existing City of London By-law CPOL.-70(b)-220 (Remuneration for Elected Officials and Appointed Citizen Members Policy). Verbatim Municipal Act sections (5, 8, 9, 11, 283, 284.11, 312) and O. Reg. 530/22 retrieved from ontario.ca and used to anchor the authority and the strong-mayor framework. Identifies the remaining items requiring City Solicitor and Treasurer action before introduction. |
| [deep-research-audit.md](deep-research-audit.md) | Perplexity Pro Deep Research comprehensive audit pass on v12 (2026-05-22, 209 sources). Confirms v12 is structurally sound. Adds findings on Municipal Conflict of Interest Act s. 4(i) (exempts all members of Council from disclosure or disqualification on PACT votes), *Auer v. Auer* (2024 SCC 36) reasonableness standard with presumption of validity for subordinate legislation, plain reading of CPOL.-70(b)-220 s. 4.2(b), and the worked example for the first PACT-driven adjustment (approximately 3.4 percentage points on January 1, 2027 from the 2026 levy increase). |
| [2025-c06-vote-record.md](2025-c06-vote-record.md) | Full verbatim per-vote tally of the November 4, 2025 London Council vote on resolution 2025-C06 (the 70th-percentile pay reset for the 2026-2030 term). Sourced directly from the official Council Minutes (eSCRIBE DocumentId 120361). Designed as a source document for campaign communications. |
| [solicitor-handoff.md](solicitor-handoff.md) | The pre-introduction handoff memo for the City Solicitor and City Treasurer. Five items requiring confirmation: counsel opinion on the override + downward-adjustment authority, Treasurer mapping of the levy figure to the rating by-law line items, plus belt-and-suspenders confirmations on Mayor coverage and MCIA s. 4(i). |

## Why these documents are in the repository

The bylaw text in [`rendered/BYLAW.md`](../rendered/BYLAW.md) is the operative instrument. Everything in this directory is the supporting record. Readers who want to argue with the bylaw can argue with it on the verifiable record. Readers who want to suggest a change can do so knowing exactly what work has already been done.
